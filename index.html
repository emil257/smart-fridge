Features

Smartfridge App
Allows you to control all the refrigerators functions directly from your smartphone.

Scannable Shelves
This feature allows the refrigerator to keep track of whats in stock, also when products are about to expire.

Built-in Cameras
The cameras inside allows you to get an overview of the contents of the refrigerator wherever you are.

Voice Assistant
With the built-in voice assistant you can ask anything, from todays weather to different food recipes.

How do I connect SmartFridge with my local grocery store?

-Register your SmartFridge on our site and then connect it via one of our collaboration companies applications/websites.

Warranty - how long?

-The warranty for SmartFridge is 5 years.

What is the delivery time of SmartFridge?

-We're shipping worldwide and delivery time is 7-21 working days depending on where you live.

When I order groceries, do I collect them at the store or do I get them delivered?

-You can either pick up the groceries at the store or have them delivered home for a small fee.

How do I register?

-You register simply by clicking the "register/sign up" button and then by following the steps.

Which companies do you collaborate with?

-We collaborate with Ica, Willys and Coop so far, we're looking to add even more companies in the future.

How does SmartFridge know what items I have stored?

-SmartFridge has a built-in item barcode scanner that scans items with a barcode. Items without barcodes, like fruits, can be put in manually.

How does SmartFridge come up with meal suggestions?

-Based on the items that's scanned and put in manually, SmartFridge then brings you suggestions of meals to cook from our collaborators databases.
